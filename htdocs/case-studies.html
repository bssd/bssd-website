Bonney & Stewart Software Development

Case Studies

Bonney & Stewart Software Development has been involved in a number of consultancy and system delivery projects.

Wealth Management

Wealth Management initiative by a large UK insurance company to provide Share Dealing and Supermarket of Funds (ISAs) online.

Bonney & Stewart Software Development were involved in the development and delivery of the Share Dealing part of the offering.

The execution of the Share Dealing was handled by a 3rd party provider via an XML link.

Structured Investments - Financial Reporting

Project for a large international financial services company involving the automated production of reports on the performance of Structured Investments.

Based on a Java architecture the system imported data from a variety of fund managers and produced reports from the aggregated results.

Invoice Matching

Project by a large Financial Software house to develop their next generation product suite.

Bonney & Stewart Software Development were involved in the development and delivery of the new Invoice Matching system.

Based on a Java architecture with a web front end. The system provided interfaces to allow orders to be imported from the procurement system or a 3rd party source.

Equity Trading - Protocol Conversion

Project for a large investment bank, involving the conversion of FIX (Financial Information eXchange) messages into a proprietary format for communication with their financial trading system.

FIX messages were read from TIBCO rendezvous messaging system. Communication with trading system was socket-based using an existing Java library.

Latency was a primary concern & the new implementation outperformed the existing solution by nearly 40%.
